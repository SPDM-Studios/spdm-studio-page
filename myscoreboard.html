My Scoreboard

MOBILE APP

Overview

My Scoreboard is a versatile and intuitive mobile application designed to help users keep track of scores for a variety of sports and games. Whether you are playing basketball, tennis, volleyball, or any casual backyard game, My Scoreboard provides a simple and clean interface to manage the game effectively.

The application features a dark-themed UI that emphasizes visibility and ease of use, ensuring that the current score is always the focal point without unnecessary distractions during gameplay.

Role

UI/UX Design, App Development

Platform

Android
